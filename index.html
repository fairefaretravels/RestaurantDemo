Clocking In & Out

1

Admin logs in → Employees tab → add staff names, roles, and 4-digit PINs.

2

Employees log in and see a large green CLOCK IN and red CLOCK OUT — always two separate buttons.

3

GPS is captured at every clock-in and clock-out and shown on the admin map as color-coded pins.

4

Admin can clock any employee in or out directly from the Clock tab.

Walkie-Talkie Radio

5

Both devices open Radio tab and allow microphone access when prompted.

6

Hold the round PTT button to transmit. Release to send to all connected team members.

7

All devices on the same company code share the channel. Change it in Employees → Settings.

Driver Tracking

8

Admin → Routes → select a driver → tap Track. Blue line draws on the map as they drive.

9

Tap Add Stop then click the map to drop numbered delivery pins.

10

Tap Optimize to get the best route with total miles and estimated time.

Reports

11

Admin → Report shows session times, GPS coords, total hours, and miles per employee.

12

Download CSV exports for payroll. Email sends a formatted summary via your mail app.

13

Deploy on Netlify for automatic HTTPS — required for GPS and microphone.
